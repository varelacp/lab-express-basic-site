Studio Ghibli - Works

Spirited Away (2001)

Spirited Away follows the story of Chihiro, a young girl who stumbles into a magical world ruled by spirits and monsters. To save her parents from becoming pigs and find a way back to the human world, Chihiro must navigate this strange and perilous realm, making allies and enemies along the way.

My Neighbor Totoro (1988)

My Neighbor Totoro is a heartwarming tale of two young sisters who move to the countryside with their father to be closer to their hospitalized mother. In the forest behind their new home, they discover a magical creature named Totoro and embark on a series of enchanting adventures.

Princess Mononoke (1997)

Princess Mononoke is a sweeping epic set in feudal Japan, where the forces of nature and the humans who seek to dominate them clash in a fierce battle for survival. The titular character, a young woman raised by wolves, finds herself caught in the middle of this conflict as she fights to protect her home and the creatures she loves.

Howl's Moving Castle (2004)

Howl's Moving Castle is a dazzling fantasy adventure about a young girl named Sophie who is transformed into an old woman by a jealous witch. To break the curse, she seeks the help of the enigmatic wizard Howl and sets off on a journey that will take her to places she never imagined.

Grave of the Fireflies (1988)

Grave of the Fireflies is a heartbreaking story set in Japan during World War II. The film follows two siblings, Seita and Setsuko, as they struggle to survive in the aftermath of the bombing of Hiroshima. With their parents gone and no one to turn to, the children must rely on each other to stay alive in a world that is falling apart around them.
